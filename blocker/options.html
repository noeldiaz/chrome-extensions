Allow only the sites you choose and block navigation to everything else.

How blocking works

When blocking is on, any top-level navigation to a site that isn’t on your allowlist is sent to a block page. Allowing a site allows its whole base domain (every subdomain). Blocking needs permission to see the pages you navigate to; Blocker asks for it the first time you start.
