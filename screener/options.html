Screener — Options

Backup & restore

Export all your settings and data to a JSON file, or import one to restore them on another machine. Importing replaces what’s on this device.

Ticket endpoint

Where the Submit ticket button sends a screenshot. Leave blank to use only Download and Copy.

What gets sent

A multipart/form-data POST with: title, description, screenshot (PNG file), page_url, and meta (JSON). The bearer token, if set, goes in the Authorization header. The token is stored locally in this browser and never sent anywhere except your endpoint.
